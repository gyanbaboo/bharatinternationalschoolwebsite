About School

Establishment

Bharat International School was established by Swatantra Seva Sansthan Sarai Nirbhay Sangramgarh, Pratapgarh, With the inspiriation of Respected Gurudev shri shri Nandiji Maharaj Aashram - JankiNagar kakarmatta kedarkhand Varanasi's Respected Bharat Singh and Respected Mata Bramharaji Kuwar

School was established on 28-09-2009 on Monday

Inauguration

School was successfully inaugrated by Respected Prof. R.P. Mishra(Former VC Allahabad University), Respected Rajkumari Ratna Singh(politician), Respected Kuwar Jayendra Pratap Singh on Dated 08-02-2011 on Tuesday.

President: Arun Kumar Singh

Organizer: Mithilesh Singh
